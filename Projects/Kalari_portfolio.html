Kuttippuram Kalari

Project information

  • Category: Static Web Page
  • Client: Kuttippuram Kalari Samajam
  • Project date: 22 September, 2023
  • Project URL: https://kuttippuramkalari-7a30c.web.app/

I am proud to present my inaugural static webpage dedicated to my cherished family community, Kalari. Merging my passion for web development and love for my community, I meticulously crafted a responsive website. It features a sleek navigation bar for easy browsing, gracefully welcoming visitors to a dedicated introduction page. An informative 'About' section sheds light on our rich heritage and values. One of the highlights is a captivating gallery, showcasing our community's vibrant journey through carefully curated visuals. Additionally, a 'Contact' page offers a seamless way to reach us, complete with an embedded map for convenience. Harnessing the power of HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, I ensured an engaging and visually appealing design. Git bash played a crucial role in version control, allowing for a streamlined and organized development process. This project marks a meaningful step in honoring my community and showcasing my web development skills.
